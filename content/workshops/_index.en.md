Tiny Tapeout offers workshops which allow people little to no experience to go through a real silicon design process,
where at the end they will be able to submit their design for manufacturing on the next Tiny Tapeout chip.

{{< figure src="images/tt-jku-workshop.jpg" title="Tiny Tapeout Workshop at Johannes Kepler University">}}

## Looking to arrange a workshop?
We offer team-led, self-led & virtual workshops so that you can tapeout no matter where you are. Check out our
offerings below and get in touch.

### Team-led workshops
Tiny Tapeout workshops are exciting and immersive sessions which introduce participants to real silicon design. In a
four-hour workshop, participants can go from drawing a transistor and building simple logic-gate circuits to creating
& submitting their own own design to be manufactured for the next Tiny Tapeout chip.

### Self-led workshops
Self-led Tiny Tapeout workshops allow educators to run their own hands-on chip design sessions with students. Using
Tiny Tapeout resources, teachers guide participants through the fundamentals of silicon design and the creation of a
simple ASIC, which can be submitted for fabrication on an upcoming Tiny Tapeout chip.
