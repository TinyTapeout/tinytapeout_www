## Launch stats

- Launched: 01 June 2026
- Submission closed: 07 July 2026
- Submitted to wafer.space using gf180mcuD 180nm open source PDK
- Chips expected October 2026

## Funding

GF180mcuD support for Tiny Tapeout was funded by [Tillitis](https://www.tillitis.se/) and [Wit](https://wit.com).

The manufacturing of Tiny Tapeout GF 0p2 silicon was funded [wafer.space](https://wafer.space) as part of their mission to support open source silicon.
