Upgrade update!

You can now see microscope images, GDS, local interconnect layers, and jump straight to project files. 

Spot something cool? Dive right into the design to see exactly how it was built.

{{< figure src="images/view.jpeg" title="GDS with project links" >}}

These are its new features:
- High-resolution microscope images of the silicon
- GDS layer visualization
- Local interconnect layer details
- Direct links to project repositories

Explore [TT07](/chips/tt07) with the new viewer [here](https://tinytapeout.github.io/tt07-chip-imaging/#url=data/tt07.json). Try out the new layer selections with the menu at the top right.

Thanks to our partners at [Texplained](https://texplained.com/) for the imaging, and thanks to Tamas for aligning the images and adding the new features.
