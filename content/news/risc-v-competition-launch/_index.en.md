Want to help build a crowdsourced microcontroller?

You're invited to design peripherals for a RISC-V chip that will be fabricated for real! 

Whether you want to design UARTs, timers, synthesizers, or any other peripheral, this is your chance to contribute to actual silicon.

And it's completely free to participate!

For full competition details, rules, and how to submit, head over to [tinytapeout.com/competitions/risc-v-peripheral/.](https://tinytapeout.com/competitions/risc-v-peripheral/)
