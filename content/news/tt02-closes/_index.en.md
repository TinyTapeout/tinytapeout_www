That's a wrap!

We ended up with [154 designs](chips/tt02/), including 10 from schools and universities still to be added. 

The range of what people built is remarkable: CPUs and microprocessors, FPGAs, sound generators, signal processors, clocks, games, and everything in between.

Here's the full TT02 [datasheet](/tt02.pdf). It's 236 pages long and documents every single project. You can browse the full list at the TT02 [repository](https://github.com/TinyTapeout/tinytapeout-02).

Thanks to everyone who submitted. More updates to come!
