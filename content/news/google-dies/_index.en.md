**Happy 2025 everyone!**

We have some cool news to kickoff the new year 🥳

We now have dies mounted in epoxy cubes from the first 5 Google-sponsored MPW runs available in the Tiny Tapeout store.

Each individually numbered chip contains different designs from the [Zero to ASIC Course](https://www.zerotoasiccourse.com/). The dies are beautifully preserved in clear epoxy resin, making them perfect for collectors or as memorable keepsakes for those who contributed to these foundational shuttles.
