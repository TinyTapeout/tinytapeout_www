[TT08](/chips/tt08) is open for submissions and we still have early-bird pricing available. 

For just $150 you can get your design manufactured and delivered on a dev board. The closing date is September 6th.

Tiny Tapeout remains the easiest and cheapest way to get to silicon!

### Demoscene Competition

The demoscene competition is running alongside [TT08](/chips/tt08). This is an opportunity for a free tapeout! All entrants get 1 free tile. If you also order a board, you'll get the graphics and sound PMODs needed for viewing all the entries.

### Infrastructure Improvements

Digital latency is now under 20ns, and we have 3.3V analog support. The infrastructure keeps getting better, and better!

Whether you're a first-time designer, a seasoned maker, or someone who's been waiting for the right moment to design a chip, [TT08](/chips/tt08) is your chance. [Get started!](https://tinytapeout.com/)
