[TT04](/chips/tt04) submissions are now closed.

The final numbers: 350 tiles total, 235 allocated and paid for, with 115 still available. 98 PCBs were allocated, 97 paid.

In total, 143 projects were submitted from over 30 countries, using 82,126 standard cells and 2,607mm of wire. 92 designs used Verilog, 46 used Wokwi, and the rest used SystemVerilog, SpinalHDL, and other HDLs.

We're now submitted to the Efabless 2309C ChipIgnite shuttle on the SkyWater 130nm open-source PDK. Chips are expected to arrive on February 2024, and PCBs on March 2024.

Thanks to everyone who submitted!
