[TT03.5](https://github.com/TinyTapeout/tinytapeout-03p5) is submitted! Both OpenLane1 and OpenLane2 versions are now with SkyWater.

TT03.5 is an experimental shuttle we ran to test the new multiplexer architecture ahead of [TT04](/chips/tt04). 

It includes 29 designs from contributors around the world. From CPUs and FPGAs to VGA clocks, a guitar tuner, a TOTP authenticator, a Simon Says game, and more. You can browse the full project index on [GitHub](https://github.com/TinyTapeout/tinytapeout-03p5).

We expect chips back in October 2023.
