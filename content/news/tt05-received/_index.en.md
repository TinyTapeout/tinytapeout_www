Tiny Tapeout 5 chips have just been received from [Efabless](https://chipfoundry.io/efabless)! 

We've already tested the chip and confirmed our test designs work. Next step is PCBA and then order fulfilment.

[TT05](/chips/tt05) launched on September 11, 2023 and closed on November 4, 2023 - with 174 designs submitted to the Efabless 2311C ChipIgnite shuttle using the SkyWater 130nm open-source PDK. 

The designs span a remarkable range from neural network models, spiking neurons, and matrix multipliers to VGA games, sound generators, UART transceivers, a Tetris implementation, a Simon Says game, a KianV uLinux SoC, and a Super Mario tune player.

Browse all 174 designs on the [chip map](https://tinytapeout.com/chips/tt05/#chip-map).
