And we're back! TT03 is now open! 

The submission repository is live on [GitHub](https://github.com/TinyTapeout/tinytapeout-03) with 250 design slots available.

We've made improvements to the scan chain since TT02. 

Sylvain 'tnt' Munaut identified that the 500 buffers in the previous scan chain were distorting the clock pulse, and suggested a fix that halves the number of buffer stages and eliminates the distortion. The result is a much cleaner, more reliable design.

Submit your design now!
