[TT04](/chips/tt04) is open for submissions!

We're running on the Efabless ChipIgnite shuttle using the SkyWater 130nm open-source PDK.

Submissions close September 8th. Head to [tinytapeout.com](https://tinytapeout.com/#submit-your-design) and submit your designs!

This is our biggest release yet. [TT04](/chips/tt04) brings the new multiplexer architecture, replacing the scan chain and delivering around three orders of magnitude faster speeds. 

Designs can now be up to 16 tiles in size, and we've added 8 bi-directional pins plus dedicated clock and reset pins on top of the existing 8 input and 8 output pins.
