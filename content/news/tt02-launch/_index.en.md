We're getting ready for the next run! The TinyTapeout-02 submission repository is now live on [GitHub](https://github.com/TinyTapeout/tinytapeout-02).

We've split the scan chain out of the user projects to make it safer, and each design now gets 150×170 microns, almost 2× the area of TT01, across 250 project slots.

The submission deadline is **Friday 2nd December at 18:00 CET.**
