This is the layout of our latest open source chip - Tiny Tapeout 9! 

By reducing cost and using open source tools, we've massively increased semiconductor accessibility.

The chip demonstrates remarkable design density and complexity. The vibrant layout visualization shows the intricate arrangement of digital logic across the die, with clear organization of power distribution networks and signal routing. 

This is a testament to how far open source silicon design has come! A fully functional ASIC designed, verified, and manufactured using freely available tools and processes.

The entire design flow from silicon to silicon has been kept open source, allowing the community to learn from and build upon every step of the tapeout process. 

This commitment to openness is what makes Tiny Tapeout a powerful platform for democratizing chip design.

For more details on the design, head over [here](/chips/tt09).
